CHANGING DISTANCE

You can switch race only while entries for the target distance are still open — once that distance closes (sold out or past 4 October), no change is possible. All changes go by email to inscripciones@costablancatrails.com and carry a €5 admin fee.

  • Moving up to a longer race: you pay the difference, at the rate in force on the day you change — not the rate you originally entered at.
  • Moving down from the 102K (or the 66K): you're refunded the difference between what you paid and the current price of the shorter race, less the €5 fee.
  • Moving down from the 41K, 20K or 17K: no money back at all.

BIB TRANSFER

You can hand your entry to another runner until 4 October, through the participant's private area. It costs €5 plus the difference between the price you paid and the price in force at the moment of transfer.

RUNNER'S BAG

102K

The GTCB 102K runner's bag contains a technical t-shirt, a Lurbel Loop 2 belt, Lurbel trail socks and the finisher medal.

  • Shirt size and model guaranteed to 10 September — enter or change after that and it's whatever stock is left.
  • Miss the collection times in the event programme and the bag waits in Elche until 30 November 2026 (venue and hours to be announced). Up to the same date you can instead have it couriered for €20 postage.

DATES THAT BITE

Three deadlines do all the damage: 7 June ends the cheap price and the 80% refund in one stroke; 10 September ends the shirt-size guarantee; 4 October closes entries, bib transfers and any refund worth having. After 31 October the entry fee is simply spent — the only exit left is the start line.
